By using this website, you accept and agree to abide by the terms and conditions mentioned in this privacy policy. You also agree to our use and disclosure of your personal information as mentioned in this privacy policy. We understand that your privacy is of paramount importance to you, so your privacy is very important to us and Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers works to ensure that the users' privacy is protected when using our service. Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers has a policy setting out how the personal information of users is processed and protected. PRODUCTS ONLINE

Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers takes care in providing elaborate information about the products that are displayed in the website. However, users may expect more information regarding the product. In such a case, members can contact our customer care or support via email or toll free number displayed prominently in the header or footer sections of the web page. We solicit your valuable feedback and suggestions.

PRODUCT AVAILABILITY

The website www.sdjewellers.com may indicate availability of a product for sale by displaying its image. However, an unforeseen situation of non-availability of the product may arise. In such a case Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers will endeavor to arrange it either from a third party or design it in any of its manufacturing units. Prompt communication will be initiated by Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers if so or even otherwise via email or phone.

PHOTOGRAPHY

While Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers exercises great care and caution while photographing the jewels and uploading them to the site (www.sdjewellers.com) to provide the users with a real-time viewing experience, possibility of any minor deviation with respect to shade, design, size due to factors such as lighting, reflection of stones, camera & sensor quality cannot be ruled out. We hope user is aware of these practical issues while placing the order with us and shall not hold Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers as accountable at a later stage.

PRODUCT CUSTOMIZATION AND PRODUCT REQUESTED BY USER

If a user requires a jewel exclusively designed to satiate his/her soul by performing modifications in existing design by adding or removing or changing the precious or semi-precious stones mounted on the ornament as such, they shall contact the sales or support team and get to know on the possibility of customization. Customization of a product is the prerogative of Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers. It may take up the order for customization or indicate inability, at its own determination. Buyer has to bear the customization cost fixed by the company.

PRODUCT PRICE

The product price is inclusive of the price of the material(s), designing or craftsmanship cost, making charges along with the wastage fee and Tax component. Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers has every right in determining the price of the product. These costs shall be referred to as ’Value Added Charges/Making Charges’.

All prices quoted and displayed in the website are in INR(Indian National Rupees). Though the price displayed in the website is the final price of the product covering price of the metal, stones, value added charges, handling charges (if applicable) and ’tax’, please be aware that the prices of jewellery fluctuate in accordance with international metal market trends and hence the final price may be subject to minor variations within the short span of making payment. In such a case, you may have to pay a bit higher or lower than the price indicated in the site. Handling charges refer to the charges we may levy in case of unanticipated practical difficulties in delivery or due to higher rates of payment to payment gateway. Check the ’Tax & Value Added Charges’ section of the terms and conditions for more clarity.

CONFIRMATION OF ORDER

An email will be sent to the member after the purchase is finalized with www.sdjewellers.com. User shall use this email as reference for their purchase.
