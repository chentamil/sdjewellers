About Us

Sri Dhanalakshmi Jewellers, established in 1960, is a trusted jewellery destination in Kanchipuram, known for purity, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction for over 60+ years.

We specialize in BIS hallmarked gold, pure silver jewellery, bridal collections, and custom-designed ornaments. Our commitment is to deliver quality, transparency, and value in every purchase.

Located near the New Railway Station, we are proud to serve generations of families with trustworthy service, fair pricing, and a wide range of traditional and modern jewellery designs.

Customers appreciate us for our friendly service, silver variety, gold purity, and reliable old gold exchange. We continue to grow with the love and support of our valued customers.

✨ Services: Gold Jewellery • Silver Jewellery • Bridal Sets • Custom Designs • Same Day Delivery Available

🏬 Experience: In-Store Shopping • Trusted Family Showroom • Customer Friendly Service
